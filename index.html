Who We Are

A Legacy of Innovation & Learning

MITS Gwalior is one of Central India's premier autonomous engineering institutions, affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV). With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a faculty of distinguished educators and researchers, we nurture talent that drives the future.

Our student-first philosophy ensures every aspect of campus life—academics, research, extracurriculars, and placements—is designed to maximize your potential.
